Difference between pillars & faith.

Pillars are the acts of worship that every muslim has to do or perform according to there ability. There are indeed conditions in it but we will talk about the usual scenarios. And the faith is of course the faith or believes in Islam.

There are total 5 pillars of islam. Which is obligatory to every muslim who has reached the age of puberty.

  1. Shahadah ( Declaration of faith )
  2. Salah ( 5 daily prayers )
  3. Zakat ( obligatory charity )
  4. Sawm ( Fasting )
  5. Hajj ( Pilgrimage )
