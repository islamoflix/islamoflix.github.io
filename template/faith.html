Faith in Islam

Assalamu alaikum(Peace be upon you)

In this section we will try to understand the faith related to islam. What are the muslim believes.

    There are total 6 pillars of faith(iman) in islam.

  1. Believe in tawheed(True monothiesm)
  2. Believe in angels
  3. Believe in the books of Allah
  4. Believe in all the messengers and prophets sent by god almighty
  5. Believe in the day of judgement and hereafter
  6. Believe in Destiny (Qadr)

Let's discuss them one by one:
